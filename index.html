The People's Academy Awards

The people have spoken (on Rotten Tomatoes) about who we think the Oscar goes to.
No critics, no Members of the Academy - just Rotten Tomatoes users.
These are the Best Pictures that had a discrepency of 10%+ between critics and users.

Bold titles are the People's Choice for that year

Green dot = Users liked it more than critics
Red dot = Users disliked it more than critics
Black rectangle represents the difference in critic % and user %
Only scores that varied 10%+ are shown in black

All data was hand sourced from Rotten Tomatoes, TMDb and the Academy Awards Database at some time before and after Academy season. These are also static from the time of entry.
